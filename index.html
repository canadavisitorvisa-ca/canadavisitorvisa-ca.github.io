Why choose a regulated RCIC?
Only licensed RCICs are legally authorized to provide immigration advice for a fee in Canada. Unregulated consultants — however cheap — put your entire immigration future at risk. Kevin is bound by the CICC Code of Professional Ethics, which means you are protected.
